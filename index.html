Things to do nearby

Randsfjorden bathing park

Randsfjorden bathing park is a local bathing park on the lake. The park is child friendly, just remember to book tickets on their site beforehand.

Kistefos museum

The Kistefos Museum is one of Europe's most important sculpture parks for contemporary art. Built on the grounds of a historical pulp mill, Kistefos today comprises an industrial museum, two art galleries and an impressive sculpture park in scenic surroundings. It has a mission to conserve the buildings and industrial heritage of the area whilst also celebrating the best of Norwegian and international contemporary art.

Hadeland Glassverk

Hadeland Glassverk is a renowned glass works with a local shop. The glass works was founded in 1762 on land belonging to the Mo estate. Production started in 1765. At the time Norway did not have the necessary skilled craftsmen, and these were recruited from abroad, principally from Germany. Initially production consisted mainly of bottles, chemists’ jars, medicine bottles and items of household glass. During the 19th century the glassworks mainly copied designs from other European countries and in the 1920s it started developing its own designs.

Parking and how to get there
If you want to drive to the venue, Thorbjørnrud Hotel has plenty of parking spaces. We recommend driving, but it's perfectly possible to take the bus. By bus, you can take bus 200 from Oslo Bussterminal to Hønefoss, and then change to bus 113 to Jevnaker.
Indoors or outdoors?
The ceremony will be held outdoors and the dinner and party indoors. We have access to the outdoor area at all times, so feel free to grab a drink at Thorbjørnrud's amazing garden at any time!
Book a room!
Thorbjørnrud Hotel has many rooms available, and has reserved rooms for our wedding. Please contact them directly to book a room, just reference that you're going to Tord and Anne-Linn's wedding. Room prices are 695,- NOK for single rooms and 895,- NOK for double, breakfast included!
Which dates should I book?
We have nothing specific planned for Friday 15th July, but we will be staying over at the hotel and it would be nice to see you if you want to spend an extra day. The main event will take place from Saturday 16th July to Sunday 17th July.
